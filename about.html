About Us

Welcome to M&R Creation Bar, where creativity and care come together to craft homemade products made with love. Founded by Rena and Megan, our mission is to bring a touch of handmade goodness into everyday life. From thoughtfully crafted beauty items to charming home essentials, every product is carefully designed and made with attention to quality, sustainability, and your happiness. At M&R Creation Bar, we believe in the power of small creations to make a big difference, and we’re excited to share our passion with you.
